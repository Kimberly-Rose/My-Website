About Inter-Act

Inter-act LIVE turns electronic crowd donations into exciting, real life events!

How it works:

Our toys are fully capable of connecting to the internet via WiFi connection. Each toy
can be programmed to turn on when a target donation amount has been met by activating
the WiFi, and entering your information and goal amount on our site. Share your toyID
or personalized QR code to enable donations. When your goal has been met, the device
will turn on! This creates an exciting and Inter-active experience that can make your
donation events memorable, and help you achieve your goal amount faster!
Toys are completely reusable, you only need to login and reset your goal amount.



Instructions:

1.  Purchase one of our exciting toys! Each toy will come equipped with full Wifi
connectivity, a personalized QR Code, a personalized link number, and access to
a support team that will swiftly resolve any issues so that your Inter-active
experience is exciting and stress free!

2.   Press the "Activate Toys" button on our home page to create a new account. When you
login you will find buttons for registering and setting up toys. Register your toy
by entering a name and the toyID that came with your device. You can check
the availability of names for your convenience. You only need to register
each toy once.

3..  To start a LIVE donation event, press the "Go LIVE" button and enter your
desired goal amount. Your toy is now ready to accept donations! Share
your toyID number or QR code with everyone who would like to donate to you.
Everyone will now be able to donate to you by pressing the "Inter-act" button
from our home page and entering your ToyID, or by directly scanning the QR Code
that is unique to your toy.

4.   Once your goal has been met, your toy will turn on for everyone to enjoy!
