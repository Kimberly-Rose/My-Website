Welcome to Inter-act Live!

This simplified website serves as an interactive demonstration of our proposed IOT donation device!

Read More
Read Less
Our concept was originally designed with streamers in mind. We wanted to create a more exciting and interactive way for them to collect donations. The streamer would have one of our toys with them on stream, which would have a goal amount set by them on our website. All toys would be pre-equipped with a distinct ID number and QR code. Once the streamer had entered a goal amount, the toy would have a status of "live" on our site, meaning anyone with the ID number or QR code could visit our site, enter either one of these values under the "Interact" tab to view the toy's custom page, and start making donations until the goal has been met. Once the goal has been met, a live donation bar on the page will be filled and the toy will activate and perform an action. The toys would be connected to WiFi and making requests to our backend to determine when to activate.

  • Press the "Reset Demo" button
  • Press the "Activate Toys" button
  • Create a new account. First and last name should be 2-40 letters in length, username 2-30 characters and password 6-25 characters with no slashes in username or password.
  • Press "Register Toy" to choose a toy name and register it. For the toy ID use either 1402 or 1403, these numbers are the working demonstration IDs.
  • Press "Go LIVE" to set a donation goal.
  • Return to the Inter-act home page by clicking the "Inter-act LIVE" button in the upper left corner.
  • Press the "Inter-Act!" button and enter the toy ID to access the toy's donation page. Note that QR codes are not enabled.
  • Keep donating to watch the bar fill up! Please make sure to click or press the buttons.
  • When the bar is full the physical device would go off. For our MVP pitch an Arduino MKR Vidor blinked a light as proof of concept.
