Diabetes Risk Survey Questions


General Health:

1.  How would you rate your general health overall?






Height and Weight:

2.  Please enter your height and weight in feet, inches, and pounds.
     Round to the nearest whole number value:







Physical Health:

3.  Approximately how many days have you been sick in the last month?




Well Being:

4.  Approximately how many days in the last month have you felt depressed,
     anxious, or just not your usual self?




Exercise:

5.  Aside from your regular job, do you participate in any physical activities
     such as running, calisthenics, golf, gardening, walking, or sports?



Sleep:

6.  On average, how many hours of sleep do you get in a 24 hour period?



Smoking:

7.  Have you smoked at least 100 cigarettes in your lifetime?



Drinking:

8.  Are you a man that drinks more than 14 drinks per week, or a
     woman that drinks more than 7 drinks per week?



Stroke:

9.  Have you ever had a stroke?



Heart Disease:

10.  Have you ever been diagnosed with Angina or Coronary Heart Disease?



Age:

11.  How old are you?







Sex:

12.  What sex are you?



Health Care:

13.  Do you have any kind of health care coverage, including health insurance,
       prepaid plans such as HMOs, government plans such as Medicare,
       or Indian Health Service?



Doctor Visits:

14.  Was there a time in the past 12 months where you needed to see a doctor,
       but were unable to because of the cost?



Income:

15.  How much is your annual income from all sources?









Education:

16.  What is the highest grade or year of school you completed?







Dental Visits:

17.  How long has it been since you visited a dentist, dental clinic,
       orthodontist, oral surgeon, or any other dental specialist?





Checkups with your general practitioner:

18.  About how long has it been since you last visited a doctor for a routine checkup?
       (A routine checkup is a general physical exam, not an exam for a specific injury,
       illness, or condition.)
