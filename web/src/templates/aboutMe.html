About Me

Hi and welcome to my site! I'm Kimberly Johnson and I'm a San Diego based Software Engineer specializing in computer system design. I have a versatile and constantly evolving skill-set which enables me to implement design solutions for a variety of computing platforms ranging from robotics and IOT devices to relational databases and websites. I'm passionate about design thinking, conscientious of the end users of my products, and always eager to learn any new technologies or languages necessary to optimize a solution or cater to preferences.
In my free time I enjoy creating icons and designs in Figma and participating in sports. I designed my own logo for this site as well as the roses, mail icon, and the SQL icon on this page. I have been practicing Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for 14 years and I am a former women's coach with international competition experience. I also enjoy indoor cycling for the best cardio workout ever and I am an animal lover and mommy of a beautiful tri-colored chihuahua named Makhmood.
