Diabetes Risk Predictor

Can we build an interactive Diabetes risk predictor using machine learning?

Using the CDC open dataset from the 2020 Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System, our team developed a model that
predicts the risk of developing Type II Diabetes from assessing a set of potential risk factors.

4 models were trained and tested, and the Logistical Regression model was selected due to its consistent performance
in each test and its highest overall F1-score.
